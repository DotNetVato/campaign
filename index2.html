Our Plan for a Safer Community

Comprehensive Public Safety Strategy

Our community deserves leadership that is committed to safety, accountability, and service. This administration will focus on proven strategies that protect families and create safer neighborhoods.

Key Initiatives

Strengthen Law Enforcement: Hire qualified deputies and invest in training and resources to ensure our officers are equipped to serve effectively.

Community Engagement: Build stronger relationships between law enforcement and the communities we serve through transparent communication and collaborative problem-solving.

Crime Prevention: Implement data-driven strategies to reduce crime, address quality-of-life issues, and create safer neighborhoods for all residents.

Support for Officers: Ensure our law enforcement professionals receive the support, training, and resources they need to do their jobs effectively.
